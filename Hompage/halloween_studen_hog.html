Hogsmeade


Introduction

Hogsmade is a wizard village, located in Scotland, England. It is the only town in England that has no muggles. According to the book, Historic Magic Historic Site, it was used as the headquarters for the 1612 Dokkaebi Rebellion, and the screaming cabin is known as the most haunted house in England. Hogwarts students are allowed to visit the school on weekends without classes, receiving a guardian's permit from the third grade. However, a ban on the visit of Hogsmith could be imposed on students who are guilty.
