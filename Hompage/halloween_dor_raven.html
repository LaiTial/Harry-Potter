Ravenclaw House-(a dormitory for the wise and considerate).


It is one of the four dormitories on Hogwarts.
The dormitory inspector is Professor Philius Flewig. The Founder's Ideology ("Let's teach only the smartest children."We value wisdom, knowledge, and wit according to... The reputation is second only to Griffindor because Slitheen accepts bad people such as pure bloodist if ambitious, and Hoppelpoo takes risks to other dormitories. Unlike the founding idea for the smartest students, there are many geeks with unusual thinking as well as students who are not just good at studying. There is also a joke that most of the people who created Ravenclo are from Gryphindor because of this eccentricity. The ghost in charge of the dormitory is a gray lady (Gray Lady) and a secret, but Helena Levonclaw, the daughter of one of Hogwarts founders. In order to enter the dormitory, you have to deal with the problems the bronze eagle poses. As the entrance to other dormitories is not password-free, but problem-solving, if it is correct, it can be entered even if it is not a member of Laban Claw. This is why students gather at the entrance of the Lavenclaw dorm to discuss the issue of eagles.
